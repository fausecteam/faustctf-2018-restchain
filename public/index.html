About Us

RESTchain allows you to leverage the full power of blockchain technology without compromising on security or efficency. Based on the the SHA-256 hash algorithm and Ed25519 signature scheme, we base our service on rock-solid and well-tested cryptographic primitives, contrary to some competitors. Using our provided client libraries, you can integrate RESTchain into your application with ease, no matter what programming language you use.

Security

In the unlikely event that you should find any security problem with our service or our website, please follow our responsible disclusure policy and don't hesitate to contact us at security@restchain.rceaas.de (you can encrypt your message using our GPG Key, Fingerprint: C2EC 7617 29D6 2843 28B6 42BA 934C D7FE 309D 55CA).
